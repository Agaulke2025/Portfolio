About Me

I am a recent graduate from The Ohio State University with a degree in Computer and Information Science. I thrive in technical and collaborative environments, excelling in adaptability and dependability to meet goals and deadlines. From May 2023 to July 2024, I worked as a Software Development Intern at The Toro Company, delivering a FOTA solution, implementing RPC calls, and developing a health monitoring system for microservices in an Agile environment. My professional experience also includes nearly three years in customer service roles at L & A Precision Lawn and Landscape and Archer's Tavern. At OSU, I served as a Teaching Assistant for the Software Components course, where I graded assignments and assisted students with complex software problems. Additionally, I was President of the Rock Climbing Club, managing risk assessment, acting as a liaison to external organizations, and coordinating event timelines.
